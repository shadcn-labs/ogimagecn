## June 23, 2026 - Shadcn Registry Components

We added 6 new OG image components designed specifically for shadcn registries: [shadcn-registry-1](/docs/components/shadcn-registry-1), [shadcn-registry-2](/docs/components/shadcn-registry-2), [shadcn-registry-3](/docs/components/shadcn-registry-3), [shadcn-registry-4](/docs/components/shadcn-registry-4), [shadcn-registry-5](/docs/components/shadcn-registry-5), and [shadcn-registry-6](/docs/components/shadcn-registry-6).

## June 10, 2026 - Shiori and Owner

We added 2 new minimal OG image components: [shiori](/docs/components/shiori) and [owner](/docs/components/owner).

## June 8, 2026 - More Components

We added 8 new OG image components: [editorial](/docs/components/editorial), [event](/docs/components/event), [logo](/docs/components/logo), [product](/docs/components/product), [profile](/docs/components/profile), [stat](/docs/components/stat), [terminal](/docs/components/terminal), and [showcase](/docs/components/showcase).

The project was also renamed from `ogcn` to `ogimagecn`.

## June 5, 2026 - Initial Release

We shipped the initial release of ogimagecn with 7 OG image components: [simple](/docs/components/simple), [grid](/docs/components/grid), [gradient](/docs/components/gradient), [blog](/docs/components/blog), [changelog](/docs/components/changelog), [quote](/docs/components/quote), and [photo](/docs/components/photo).

Follow [ogimagecn on GitHub](https://github.com/shadcn-labs/ogimagecn) for source history and future tagged releases.
